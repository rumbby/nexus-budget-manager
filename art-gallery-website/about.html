Who We Are

Established in 1985, LuxeGems has grown from a small family workshop to an internationally renowned jewelry house. Our New York flagship boutique continues our tradition of excellence in fine jewelry craftsmanship and design.

With three generations of master jewelers, we combine time-honored techniques with innovative design to create pieces that become family heirlooms. Each LuxeGems creation tells a story of passion, precision, and unparalleled beauty.

Our Collections

LuxeGems offers exquisite diamond jewelry, precious gemstone creations, and custom-designed pieces. Our collections range from timeless classics to contemporary designs, all crafted with the finest materials and exceptional attention to detail.

We source only the most exceptional diamonds and gemstones, each hand-selected for its quality, brilliance, and character. Our master jewelers then transform these treasures into wearable works of art.

Our Promise

To create jewelry of uncompromising quality that celebrates life's most precious moments. We are committed to ethical sourcing, exceptional craftsmanship, and personalized service that exceeds expectations. Each LuxeGems piece is designed to be treasured for generations.
