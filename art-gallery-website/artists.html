About The Artist

Erlmy henstion is a celebrated jewelry designer renowned for her exquisite diamond creations and innovative metalwork. Born into a family of jewelers in St. Petersburg, Russia, she moved to New York in 2005 to establish her eponymous jewelry line. Her designs explore the interplay of light and structure, often incorporating intricate platinum settings and rare colored diamonds that create depth and movement in each piece. Elena's signature style has been described as "wearable poetry in precious metals and gems.".

Her work explores the intersection of memory, emotion, and the subconscious, often incorporating mixed media elements that add texture and depth to her paintings. Elena's unique style has been described as "a visual symphony of color and form."

She holds an MFA from the School of Visual Arts in New York and has exhibited her work internationally in galleries and museums.

Artist Statement

"My work is an exploration of the unseen emotional landscapes that exist beneath the surface of our conscious awareness. Through bold strokes and vibrant colors, I seek to capture the energy and movement of the human psyche."

"Each jewelry is a journey into the unknown, where intuition guides my hand and the canvas becomes a record of that exploration. I work primarily with acrylics and mixed media, building layers that reveal the history of the painting's creation."

"My hope is that viewers will find their own stories within these abstract forms, connecting with the work on a deeply personal level."

Erlmy henstion
